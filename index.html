Welcome to the Intermountain Spine Institute

We appreciate your visit

The founders of the Intermountain Spine Institute introduced the concept of multi-disciplinary spine care to the Intermountain West in 1991. We approach spine care from a collaborative, holistic point of view. Combining state of the art medical techniques with emphasis on physical and emotional well being results in better patient outcomes and lower total cost.

It is our mission to provider the most current and effective spine care, delivered with compassion, efficient and expertise. Thorough medical evaluation paired with cutting edge diagnostic technology helps us accomplish this mission.
