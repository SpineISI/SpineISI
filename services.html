Our Services

ISI Philosophy

The Intermountain Spine Institute introduced the concept of multi-disciplinary spine care to the intermountain west in 1990. We approach spine care from a holistic point of view, combining state of the art medical techniques with emphasis on physical and emotional well being. The result is that patients receive the tools that allow them to manage their own care for life, rather than providing short term relief for a particular episode.

ISI Medical Services
RAPID EVALUATION & CARE

ISI has partnered with Intermountain Healthcare to create an innovative back care clinic, located at the TOSH Medical Tower. This clinic allows us to offer same day rapid evaluation and care for acute back conditions. Patients with conditions requiring it can be efficiently transferred to ISI for additional work up and care. Contact the TOSH Back Care Clinic at (801) 314-2210.

COMPREHENSIVE MANAGEMENT

Our conservative providers provide timely, thorough evaluation, diagnostic workups and therapeutic treatment of your condition. We efficiently manage acute, sub-acute, and chronic spine conditions. Complete evaluation and timely diagnosis is followed by thorough patient education and effective treatment.

DIGITAL RADIOLOGY

ISI offers a digital radiology suite onsite, making the process of obtaining X-ray evaluation effortless. Your films are transmitted to our providers electronically, and can be transferred to digital media while you wait. This allows you to keep a personal archival copy.

ADVANCED IMAGING

ISI has partnered with several local imaging centers, allowing us to offer rapid access to advanced imaging modalities such as CT, MRI, and bone scan. In some cases same day delivery is available.

PHYSICAL THERAPY

ISI maintains close ties to a network of physical therapists and rehabilitation centers in the intermountain west. These relationships ensure that you receive the best quality therapy for your condition.

Interventional Therapy

ISI maintains an onsite interventional procedure room, and offers a broad range of interventional procedures to our patients. Please visit our education page for more information.

SURGICAL CARE

Our orthopedic surgeons offer the state of the art in proven spine surgical techniques. Surgeries are performed on-campus at TOSH, allowing the best in integrated care. Pre-operative, peri-operative, and post-operative care is all available in one center, offering convenience and maximimum continuity of care.

BEHAVIORAL MEDICINE

Spine problems are not just physical phenomena. Proper management requires integration of the emotional and social aspects of the condition as well. ISI offers access to clinical social workers as well as licensed psychologists to assist you in managing these aspects of spine care.
